Real Value LA & OC

A dynamic web app for visualizing data across Los Angeles and Orange counties. Users who are looking to relocate or are just curious about their surroundings will benefit from the data presented.


RealValue provides an area score derived from calculating the standard deviation from each data set (average home value, violent crime frequency, job availability) and adding them together. The RealValue score is shown on a color scale and on mouse hover; the score adjusts in real-time according to user selections. On zoom, clusters of restaurants, parks, and schools are shown with individual place details on click.


Key Technologies:

  • AngularJS
  • Angular Material
  • Angular Leaflet & geoJSON
  • D3.js
  • Firebase
  • AJAX (Zillow API, Glassdoor API, Google Places API)
